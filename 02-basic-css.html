Copa Lucioball

A Gamemode on Overwatch.

Copa Lucioball a gamemode inside of Overwatch which only comes out either During the NewYears Event or Summer Event, but mostly in the Summer Event.

The gamemode makes you play as only 1 hero "Lucio", A Music loving skater and there are 2 teams opposing eachother with 3 players on each side where the players have to either Punch or "Boop" the ball to the opposing team's goal.
